Tournament Rules

  1. Ali and Keshia can change the rules at any time
  2. Ali and Keshia can take away or add points to anyone at any time
  3. Ranked games are scored with standard competition ranking ("1224")
  4. Ties for prizes will be solved with sudden death rounds (or just rock-paper-scissors)
  5. Players will be grouped based on current score for team-based games. (ie: first with last)
  6. We're here to have fun, keep it chill!

Awards

  • Overall 1st Place: The player with the most points the whole night
  • Overall 2nd Place: The player with the second most points the whole night
  • Overall 3rd Place: The player with the third most points the whole night
  • Middle of the Pack: The player who landed perfectly in the middle of all players. (If tie, top player wins)
  • Second to Last: The player with the second to last points the whole night
  • Master of Mayhem: The player most likely to cause chaos
  • Unshakeable: The player who was the most calm under pressure
  • Daredevil: The player who made the most daring or risky moves
  • Strategic Saboteur: The player who makes the game harder for others
  • Team Captain: The player who best supports their teammates
  • Button Smasher: The player who is mostly just smashing random buttons
  • Jester: The player with the funniest moments
  • Joyful Gamer: The player that just came to have fun the most
